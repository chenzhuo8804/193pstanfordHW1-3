This is the Assignment1 for iPad and iPhone development by Stanford University (CSE 193p http://www.stanford.edu/class/cs193p/cgi-bin/drupal/) . The product of this assignment is a calculator.

Asst1 implemented a calculator. The calculator uses a stack to manage it operands, so if you want to calculator 3+5 , you will first press 3,press enter, then press 5, then press +. The Enter button would press 3 into the stack and the + would pop the operands off the stack.
